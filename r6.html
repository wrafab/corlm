Project Description:


1. Printing Stickers!
The first assignment of this rotataion was to make two stickers with the Roland GS-24 CAMM 1 in one color. The, using the Roland 540i printer, we had to contour and cut colorful stickers. To design all the stickers, I used Corel Draw.

I printed two, one colored stickers and put them on my notebook. With the remaining sticker paper, I cut out a heart and M. Printing was a little difficult because I had to make sure the printedr cut the paper a proper amount of times to cut the design onto the sticker, but not cut the paper itself.

Next, I printed out the colored stickers. This is when I used the contour tool on Corel Draw so that it would cut the sticker out in a circle. I was very excited when these printed out, especially because it was fast and easy.


Creation of each sticker...
- I created all the stickers using Corel Draw and saved them as .eps
- Each sticker I made is related to me in some way or is something I like.
- The most challenging part was finding a picture that had minimum detail to print from the Roland GS-24 CAMM without any issues.
- I had never used these machines for printing stickers before, so that was something new that I learned.
