Rotation 8: Soldering


Project Description:

At first, I thought soldering would not be very insteresting, but when I started doing it, I found it to be very fun and relaxing! The process of soldering is simple if you follow the instructions correct. However, if you make one mistake in the placetment of a piece, the overall circut would not work.
I soldered three different boards. The first had six lights that chased on another down a line. The second was a strobe light. And the last soldering porject was a sound of light, where when you clapped the light would turn on. The only one that did not work for me was the last one.
