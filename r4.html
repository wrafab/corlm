Rotation 4: Laser and Vinyl Cutter


Project Description:

For this rotation, we learned the difference between raster and vectors. To raster means to etch the design on the cardboard or material you are using, vector is to cut through the material to make the design. First, using the MakerCase download I cut out a small box made of cardboard with designs that described me on each face of the box. I used CorelDraw to make the file and printing it with the laser cutter was easy. I took the six pieces of the box home and glued them together. Next, I created three different shapes on CorelDraw with cut outs on them so that when put together, they fit snuggly to make a structure. I drew a few rectangles on each shape for the spot that would be cut out at as a slit for placing the different shapes together.
